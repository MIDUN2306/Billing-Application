## Color Scheme

### Status Colors
- **Green (#10B981)**: Active status
- **Gray (#6B7280)**: Inactive status
- **Red (#EF4444)**: Delete/Deactivate actions
- **Blue (#3B82F6)**: Transfer actions

### Role Colors
- **Red (#DC2626)**: Admin role
- **Blue (#2563EB)**: Manager role
- **Green (#059669)**: Staff role

### UI Colors
- **Primary (#8B1A39)**: Main brand color (buttons, active states)
- **Secondary (#1A1A1A)**: Text and borders
- **Background (#FFFFFF)**: Main background
- **Gray (#F9FAFB)**: Secondary background

## Interaction States

### Buttons
- **Default**: Solid color with text
- **Hover**: Slightly darker shade
- **Active**: Pressed state
- **Disabled**: Grayed out with reduced opacity

### Cards
- **Default**: White background with border
- **Hover**: Shadow elevation
- **Selected**: Border highlight

### Inputs
- **Default**: Gray border
- **Focus**: Primary color ring
- **Error**: Red border
- **Disabled**: Gray background
