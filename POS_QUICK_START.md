# POS System - Quick Start Guide

## ðŸš€ Getting Started

### Step 1: Access the POS
1. Log in to your account
2. Click **"POS"** in the sidebar
3. The new POS interface will load

### Step 2: Browse Products
1. **View All Products**: Scroll through the product grid
2. **Filter by Category**: Click category pills at the top
   - All, Beverages, Snacks, Puff & Cakes, Bun, etc.
3. **Search**: Type product name or SKU in search bar

### Step 3: Add Items to Cart
1. Click on any product card
2. Item is added to cart with quantity 1
3. Toast notification confirms addition
4. Cart updates automatically

### Step 4: Manage Cart

#### Desktop View (Right Sidebar)
- **Increase Quantity**: Click `+` button
- **Decrease Quantity**: Click `-` button
- **Manual Quantity**: Type number in quantity field
- **Add Discount**: Enter amount in discount field
- **Remove Item**: Click `X` button
- **Clear All**: Click "Clear" button in cart header

#### Mobile View (Bottom Bar)
- Shows total amount and item count
- Click "Checkout" to proceed to payment

### Step 5: Select Customer (Optional)
1. Click **User icon** in header
2. Search for customer or select "Walk-in"
3. Customer info appears below search bar
4. Click `X` to remove customer

### Step 6: Proceed to Payment
1. Click **"Proceed to Payment"** button
2. Review order items and totals
3. Select payment method:
   - **Cash**: Enter amount received, see change
   - **Card**: Direct payment
   - **UPI**: Direct payment
4. Click **"Complete Sale"**

### Step 7: Receipt
- Receipt window opens automatically
- Print or save as needed
- Sale is recorded in system

## ðŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### Products Not Loading
- Check internet connection
- Refresh page (F5)
- Click refresh button in POS
- Verify you're logged in

### Cart Not Updating
- Clear browser cache
- Reload page
- Check for JavaScript errors in console

### Payment Not Completing
- Verify all required fields
- Check payment amount
- Ensure sufficient permissions
- Try different payment method

### Receipt Not Printing
- Check browser pop-up settings
- Allow pop-ups for this site
- Try different browser
- Use manual print (Ctrl+P)
