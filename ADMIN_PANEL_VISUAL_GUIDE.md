## Best Practices

1. **Always assign users to stores** (except super admins)
2. **Use descriptive store names** for easy identification
3. **Set appropriate roles** based on responsibilities
4. **Deactivate instead of delete** to maintain audit trail
5. **Use search and filters** for large user/store lists
6. **Test on mobile** before deploying to staff
