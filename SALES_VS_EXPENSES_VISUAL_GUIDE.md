"Are our expenses too high?"
- Compare red bar heights across days

"When do we make the most sales?"
- Find days with tallest green bars
- Replicate successful strategies

"What's our minimum daily sales target?"
- Average expense bar height = minimum target
- Ensure green bars consistently exceed red bars
- Adjust pricing if needed
