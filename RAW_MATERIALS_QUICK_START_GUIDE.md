## How to Add Raw Materials

### Adding a Making Product (Ingredient)

1. Go to **Raw Materials** page
2. Click **"Add New Material"**
3. Select **"Making Product"** (default)
4. Enter the material name (e.g., "Milk", "Tea Powder")
5. Click **"Add Material"**

**Note**: SKU field is hidden for making products

### Adding a Ready to Use Product

1. Go to **Raw Materials** page
2. Click **"Add New Material"**
3. Select **"Ready to Use"**
4. Enter the product name (e.g., "Biscuits", "Samosa")
5. Enter the **SKU/Item Code** (e.g., "9", "15", "37")
6. Click **"Add Material"**

**Note**: SKU is mandatory for ready-to-use products

## Stock Card Display

When viewing raw materials, each card now shows:

### For Making Products
- Material name
- **Blue badge**: "Making Product"
- Quantity and unit
- Purchase price
- Stock status

### For Ready to Use Products
- Product name
- **SKU code** displayed below name
- **Purple badge**: "Ready to Use"
- Quantity and unit
- Purchase price
- Stock status

## Adding Stock

The process is the same for both types:

1. Click **"Add Stock"** or **"Refill"** on a material card
2. Select the raw material
3. Enter quantity and unit
4. Enter purchase price (per unit or total)
5. Save

The system automatically tracks purchase history in the **Purchase Logs** tab.

## Tips

### For Making Products
- Use these for recipe-based products
- Track ingredients like milk, tea, coffee, sugar
- Monitor usage in production

### For Ready to Use Products
- Use these for purchased finished goods
- Always include the SKU code for easy identification
- Track inventory levels for reordering
- SKU helps match with supplier invoices

## Common Workflows

### Workflow 1: Adding Tea Ingredients
1. Add "Tea Powder" as Making Product
2. Add "Milk" as Making Product
3. Add "Sugar" as Making Product
4. Add stock for each ingredient
5. Use in tea production recipes

### Workflow 2: Managing Biscuits Inventory
1. "Biscuits" already exists with SKU: 9
2. Click "Add Stock" or "Refill"
3. Enter quantity received (e.g., 50 packets)
4. Enter purchase price
5. Track sales and reorder when low

### Workflow 3: Receiving Supplier Delivery
1. Check supplier invoice for SKU codes
2. Find matching products in your inventory
3. Click "Refill" on each product
4. Enter quantities and prices from invoice
5. System logs the purchase automatically

## Need Help?

- **Making Products**: For ingredients you use to make beverages
- **Ready to Use**: For finished products you buy and resell
- **SKU**: Item code from your supplier's catalog
- **Stock Status**: Automatically calculated based on quantity
