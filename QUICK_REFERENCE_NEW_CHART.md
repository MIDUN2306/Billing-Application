- Look for ways to increase sales even more
- Maintain expense control

### If You See Loss (Red Badge) âŒ
- **Option 1**: Reduce expenses (red bar)

  - Reduce waste

- **Option 2**: Increase sales (green bar)

  - Increase prices (if possible)
  - Attract more customers

## Common Questions

**Q: Why is my red bar so high?**
A: You're spending a lot on petty cash and raw materials. Review your expenses.

**Q: Why is my green bar so low?**
A: Your sales are low. Focus on selling more or increasing prices.

**Q: Both bars are small, is that bad?**
A: Not necessarily. If green > red, you're still profitable. But you might want to grow your business.

**Q: Can I see daily breakdown?**
A: This chart shows totals. For daily details, check the modals by clicking on the cost cards above.
